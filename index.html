Information We Collect

Area Code Blocker requires access to the following permissions to function:

Required Permissions

  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): To check if incoming callers are in your contact list and exempt them from blocking
  • Phone State (READ_PHONE_STATE): To detect incoming calls in real-time
  • Call Log (READ_CALL_LOG): To maintain a history of blocked calls within the app
  • Post Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To show you notifications when calls are blocked

Optional Permissions

  • Add Contacts (WRITE_CONTACTS): Only used when you manually choose to add a blocked number to your contacts from the block list
